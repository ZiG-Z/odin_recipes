BLT

This BLT is classic: bacon, lettuce, and tomato

  • 4 slices bacon
  • 2 leaves lettuce
  • 2 slices tomato
  • 2 slices bread, toasted
  • 1 tablespoon mayonnaise
  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Cook bacon in a large deep skillet
  3. Arrange cooked bacon
  4. Close to make a sandwich
  5. Serve and enjoy
